FAQ & Travel Tips

Helpful information for a smooth island visit, plus a contact form for traveler questions.

Travel Tips

  • Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.
  • Alcohol is not served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • The drinking age on Taniti is 18.
  • Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, while rural areas use less English.
  • There is one hospital and several clinics with multilingual employees.
  • Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, though many businesses also accept euros and yen.
  • Major credit cards are accepted in many locations.
Plan your stay around activities in Taniti City and nearby beaches.
