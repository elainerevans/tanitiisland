Taniti Lodging

Choose from island resorts, family-owned hotels, cozy bed & breakfasts, and budget-friendly hostels.

Hotels & Resorts

  • Taniti Hostel — Starting at $25 a night.
  • Luminous Hotel — Family-owned hotel with breakfast included.
  • Paradise Hotel — Rooms starting at $75 a night.
  • Super 6 — First family-owned hotel on Taniti.
  • Beach Island — Family-owned hotel on a private beach.
  • Three Seasons — Beach side, four-star resort.

Bed & Breakfasts

  • M & M Bed & Breakfast — Minutes from the beach and city.
  • Arcadia B&B — Across from the arcade, family favorite.
  • Iris Family B&B — First bed and breakfast on Taniti.
  • Mary’s B&B — Homemade bagels and fresh coffee.
Private guesthouses and bed & breakfasts offer a quiet stay near nature.
Beachfront resorts place you steps away from turquoise water.
