Taniti Transportation

Travel to the island by air or cruise, then explore by bus, taxi, rental car, bike, or boat.

To the Island

The most popular way to travel to Taniti is by air via small propeller plane or jet. Small cruise ships dock in Yellow Leaf Bay one night per week.

Within the Island

  • Public buses serve Taniti City from 0500 to 2300.
  • Private buses serve the rest of the island.
  • Taxis are available through the Taniti Taxi app.
  • Rental cars are available at the airport.
  • Bikes can be rented from local vendors.
