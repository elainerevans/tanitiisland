Taniti Activities

Enjoy restaurants, museums, tours, volcano views, beaches, shopping, and the energy of Taniti City.

Restaurants

Pal’s Fish Hut, Taniti Beach, Pacific Tides, Ocean Breeze, Bay Shores Seafood, Blue Ribbon Café, Liberty Diner, Redwood Grill, Foreign Flavors, and The Giant Pan.

Entertainment

Taniti History Museum, chartered fishing tours, snorkeling tours, zipline tours, beach dance club, movie theater, history art gallery, bowling center, brewery, and island pubs.

Popular Sightseeing

  • Taniti City
  • Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Multiple white sand beaches
  • Hiking in the rainforest
  • Snorkeling and zip-lining
  • Bus and boat tours around the island
  • Taniti’s active volcano
